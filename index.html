Who We Are

Island Admirals

The biggest organised Vasco football fans outside Brazil!

Since 2019 we support Vasco da Gama by honoring the maltese cross and showing respect to everyone as our flag suggests.

Our goals are to create the biggest Vasco community where we could all support each other, have fun and share good memories on behalf of a common love.

If you looking to share this love with us and have some fun, come join us .
